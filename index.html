The Learn Enough Story

Learn enough to be dangerous is a leader in the movement to teach technical sophistication, the seemingly magical abiliy to take command of your commputer and get it to do your bidding. This includes everything from command lines and coding to guessig keyboard shortcuts, Googling error messages, and knowing when to just reboot the darn thing. We believe that there are at least a billion people that can benefit from learning technical sophistication, probably more. To join our movement sign up through our official email list now!

Background
